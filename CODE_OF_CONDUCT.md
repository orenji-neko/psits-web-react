## üíô Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and inclusive community for all students, developers, contributors, administrators, and users of the PSITS platform. We value collaboration, respect, honesty, integrity, and professionalism in all our interactions.

Our community is guided by the values of the University of Cebu and the College of Computer Studies:

- **Quality Education** - We strive for excellence in everything we do
- **Industry Leadership** - We maintain professional standards and innovation
- **Social Responsibility** - We are committed to serving our community with integrity
- **Honesty & Integrity** - We conduct ourselves with truthfulness and moral uprightness
- **Professionalism** - We maintain high standards in all communications and conduct

## ‚úÖ Expected Behavior

We encourage all community members to:

- **Be Welcoming & Inclusive** - Create a friendly environment for everyone regardless of background, experience level, or identity. Use inclusive language and welcome diverse perspectives.
- **Be Respectful** - Treat all members with kindness and respect. Value differences of opinion and approach.
- **Be Collaborative** - Work together constructively. Share knowledge, support each other's growth, and celebrate each other's contributions.
- **Be Professional** - Maintain professionalism in all communications, code contributions, and project-related activities.
- **Be Honest & Accountable** - Take responsibility for your work and actions. Communicate transparently and report issues or concerns promptly.
- **Respect Privacy & Security** - This platform handles sensitive student data including personal information, payment details, and attendance records. Always prioritize user privacy, data security, and confidentiality. Follow all security protocols and best practices.
- **Maintain Confidentiality** - Respect organizational information, project details, and any confidential data. Do not share sensitive information without proper authorization.

## ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments based on any protected characteristic
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Bullying or intimidation of any community member
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sexual harassment, unwelcome sexual attention, or advances
- Unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of student data or confidential information
- Data breaches or violations of security protocols
- Reverse engineering, decompiling, or attempting to access code/systems without authorization
- Unprofessional conduct that undermines community values
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional educational setting

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement & Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct will be reviewed and addressed appropriately. Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, consequences may include:

**For Minor Violations:**

- Private discussion and warning
- Request to modify or remove problematic contribution
- Temporary restriction from participation

**For Serious Violations:**

- Removal from project team
- Revocation of access to platform and project resources
- Permanent ban from community

**For Critical Violations (Security/Data Breaches):**

- Immediate revocation of all access
- Escalation to university authorities if applicable
- Legal action if proprietary information or student data is compromised
